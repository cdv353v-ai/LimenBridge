Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

If LimenBridge isn't right for you, we'll refund your payment in full — no questions asked — within 7 days of purchase.

1. Free trial

All new accounts begin with a 3-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You will not be charged unless you actively choose to continue with a paid plan.

2. Refund window

If you purchase a Monthly or Annual plan and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of the payment date. After 7 days, payments are non-refundable.

For Annual plans, the 7-day window applies from the date of the annual payment — not from the end of each month.

3. How to request a refund

Send an email to hello@limenbridge.cc with the subject line Refund request. Include the email address associated with your account. We will process the refund within 5 business days.

Refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe. Depending on your bank, it may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

4. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

To cancel, contact us at hello@limenbridge.cc and we will cancel your subscription and confirm by email.
