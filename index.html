Questions before you decide

How is this different from Calm or Headspace?
There are apps that promise 500 relaxation techniques. LimenBridge is one button. We removed everything else, because in the evening, choosing is already a load. Calm and Headspace are broad platforms with something for everyone, any time of day. LimenBridge does one thing: a structured morning and evening track, timed to your body's natural rhythms.
What's actually inside the programme?
A 28-day cycle of morning and evening audio sessions built around guided breathing and music, plus a mood diary and progress graph from day one.
Where is my data stored?
Your listening history and mood ratings are stored locally on your device. We don't hold a database, sell data, or run ads.
What if it doesn't work for me?
Listen to both tracks in the demo before you commit to anything. No auto-renewal — you only ever pay for the period you choose.
