How it works

Two states.
Two tracks.

The way you feel throughout the day isn't random. It follows a biological pattern. LimenBridge is built around that pattern — morning and evening tracks are different because your body is in a different state at each end of the day.

☼

Morning

So the day doesn't begin with anxiety already waiting

Around thirty minutes after waking, your body shifts from sleep into the day. That's a natural transition — not a stress response. The morning track is quiet and grounding. It works with that moment rather than against it.

☾

Evening

So sleep comes more easily

By evening your body is ready to wind down. But tension, screens, and the noise of the day often get in the way. The evening track is slower and deeper. It helps the transition happen.

From Mark

Why this exists

A few years ago I couldn't sleep. I'd lie awake replaying the day — which was strange, given that helping people with anxiety is what I do. But I couldn't seem to fix my own.

One evening my wife mentioned something she'd found — breathing exercises with specific music. I was sceptical. Within a week, sleep started coming back.

Over thirty years of working with people, I kept seeing the same thing: someone finds a practice, a tool, some support — and then they're on their own in the gap. No structure. Nothing to signal that things are okay. Tension creeps back in the evenings. Mornings feel heavy. Thoughts won't settle.

People need something to hold the nervous system steady. The same thing that helped me.

That's why LimenBridge exists.

Before you start

LimenBridge is designed for a specific kind of person. It works best when you know whether that's you.

It's for

People who want daily structure for their nervous system — something reliable to return to, morning and evening, day in, day out.

It's not for

Anyone in acute crisis or experiencing suicidal thoughts. If that's where you are right now, please reach out to a professional or your local emergency services.

Questions before you decide

How is this different from Calm or Headspace?

Calm and Headspace are broad platforms — meditation, sleep stories, breathwork, focus music. Something for everyone, any time of day.

LimenBridge does one thing: a structured morning and evening track, timed to your body's natural rhythms. No library to browse, no decisions to make — the right track is simply there when it's time.

What's actually inside the programme?

A 28-day cycle of morning and evening audio sessions built around guided breathing and music structured to work with your body's natural rhythms.

The programme uses 14 tracks, heard twice. Not because we ran short of material — but because the second time you hear a track, your nervous system has already begun to shift. The same audio lands differently when you're in a different state.

Days 29 and 30 have no tracks — just two short reflection questions. A pause for your nervous system to register what's changed before the next cycle begins.

Your progress graph is available from the first day — 28 days of morning and evening mood, building day by day.

Where is my data stored?

Your listening history and mood ratings are stored locally on your device. We don't hold a database, sell data, or run ads. If you change devices or clear your browser, that history won't transfer — syncing across devices is on our roadmap.

What if it doesn't work for me?

Listen to both tracks in the sandbox before you commit to anything. After starting the programme, you can cancel with a full refund within 7 days.
