How it works

The way you feel throughout the day isn't random. It follows a biological logic. LimenBridge is built around it: the morning track and the evening track are different, because morning and evening are different states of your body.

Morning

About thirty minutes after waking, the body shifts from sleep into the day. This is a natural rise — not stress. The morning track is gentle, no sharp sounds. It works with that moment: helps you start the day without anxiety at the door.

Evening

By evening the body is ready to shift into recovery. But accumulated tension, screens, and news disrupt that transition. The evening track is slower, deeper. It helps the shift happen — so you fall asleep with less anxiety and wake a little lighter.

One day with LimenBridge

When you're anxious or tired, choosing what to listen to is one more effort. LimenBridge removes that choice: the right thing arrives at the right time.

  • Your morning

    Your calendar reminds you — at the time you set yourself. Open the track. A few minutes. Afterwards, one simple question: How are you right now? Not a journal entry, not an analysis — just a note to yourself.

  • During the day

    The whole day is yours. Work, people you care about — everything that makes up an ordinary life.

  • Your evening

    Your calendar reminds you again. A track and one short question: How did the day go? Two minutes, no more.

  • 14 days

    LimenBridge remembers your answers. Not to judge or analyze. To show you, two weeks in, a simple graph: how your state shifted morning and evening, day by day.
