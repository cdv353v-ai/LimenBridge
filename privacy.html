Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LimenBridge is a wellness audio program. We collect only what is necessary to deliver the service. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

1. Who we are

LimenBridge is operated by Mark Vdovskikh, based in Portugal (European Union). The website is available at limenbridge.cc.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR, we are the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

Contact: hello@limenbridge.cc

2. What data we collect and why

Email address

When you register, we collect your email address. We use it to send you access to the program and occasional service-related messages. Legal basis: performance of a contract (delivering the service you signed up for).

Name

Optional. If you provide it, we use it to personalise messages. Legal basis: legitimate interest (improving communication quality).

Onboarding responses

During setup, we ask about your schedule (wake and sleep times) and how you're feeling. This information is stored locally in your browser only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Mood log

Your daily mood check-ins are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). They do not leave your device. We do not have access to this data.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Push notification token

If you grant permission, we store a Firebase Cloud Messaging token to send you daily reminders. This token is stored locally in your browser. You can revoke permission at any time through your device settings.

3. How we store your data

Your email address is stored with MailerLite, our email service provider, located in the United States. MailerLite participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. Their privacy policy is available at mailerlite.com.

All other personal data — mood logs, schedule preferences, listening history — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and we have no access to it.

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your email address for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove it within 30 days.

Local browser data (mood log, preferences) remains on your device until you clear your browser storage or uninstall the app.

5. Your rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@limenbridge.cc. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the EU, you may also contact the Portuguese data protection authority (CNPD) at cnpd.pt. If you are in the UK, you may contact the ICO at ico.org.uk.

6. California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • The right to delete personal information we hold about you
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information

We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@limenbridge.cc.

7. Children

LimenBridge is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of LimenBridge after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
